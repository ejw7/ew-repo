Sweet As Honey!

Honey Bees and Why They're So Great

  1. They polinate plants. Plants that produce fruits, beautiful flowers, herbs, cotton, and so many other plants too!
  2. They make honey! Delicious honey that's not only good in you tea, it also has microbs that help your immune system.
  3. They're fuzzy little balls of goodness!

How You Can Help Your Local Honey Bees

Let's keep these bees happy and healthy!

  • Plant a variety of flowers and plants for the bees to pollinate.
  • Plant things bees like! That includes clover, mints, asters, and lots of other wildflowers.
  • Stop using pesticides!
